Typing Online

Discover the Typing World

This is an English-language website designed to help you become faster and more accurate at typing on a computer. Our goal is to reduce your typing errors and increase your typing speed.

Created by:

Fagilah ANDRIANJAKA

Fenohasina RA-FANOMEZANA

We are first-year students at HEI (Haute École d'Informatique), in the common core program. We created this website to help ourselves and other students like us—or even people who already have a job and want to improve their soft skills. It's also great for anyone who is simply curious and wants to find out how good they are at typing.

How does it work?

You can click Play in the navigation menu and choose between typing
full books or completing challenges by selecting different stages.

Do I need an account?

No, you don’t need to create an account to play our games.
However, having an account allows you to save your progress and pick up
right where you left off.
